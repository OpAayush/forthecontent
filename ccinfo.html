Creator Crew

What Is Creator Crew?

Creator Crew is a give and receive support system for YouTube content creators. It will allow you to gain more views, likes, and watch time on your videos, while also giving back the same type of support to your fellow content creators

Certain video interactions and analytics such as likes and watch time are proven to be helpful when it comes to having your videos shown to others, in places like the home page and recommended list

In short, you will post a link to your video, other people will watch and like your video and you will do the same for them in return

  • Liking videos (click to expand)

    • Open each video in your queue on YouTube by clicking on the video's title, then like the video
      Once you have liked the videos, refresh the queue page and you will see the (like indicator) below the video will be green

  • Watching videos (click to expand)

    • Play each video in your queue for a miniumum of 10 minutes. If a video is shorter than 10 minutes you only need to watch the full video once
      Once the minimum watch time is complete, the (status indicator) will turn green
      You can now refresh the queue page and check that any completed videos were removed from your queue successfully

Requirements

  • You must watch your queue at least once every 3 days - videos older than 3 days will be flagged and you may be removed from the group without notice
  • You must like, and watch each video for a minimum of 10 minutes for it to be removed from your queue
  • You must not skip or seek through any portion of the video
  • You must notify a staff member if you are unable to meet these requirements before hand

Why Do I Need To Sign In?

Discord sign in is required in order to verify you are actually a member of the server and that you have the Creator Crew role
Google sign in is required in order to confirm you are meeting the requirements of being in Creator Crew, such as completing your watch time and liking videos
